Sports Based on Anime

The term "sports" refers to a wide range of physical activities and games that are often competitive and governed by a set of rules or customs. These activities usually involve individual or team participation and can be played for enjoyment, recreation, or competition.

American Football

A team sport played between two teams of eleven players each, focusing on advancing an oval-shaped ball into the opposing team's end zone to score points.

Tennis

A sport played between two players (singles) or two teams of two players each (doubles), where the aim is to hit the ball over the net into the opponent's court.

Basketball

A game played between two teams of five players each, aiming to score points by shooting a ball through the opposing team's hoop.

Soccer (Football)

Known as the world's most popular sport, it's played by two teams of eleven players each on a rectangular field with a goal at each end.

Volleyball

A game played between two teams of six players each, where the goal is to send the ball over the net and make it touch the ground on the opponent's side of the court.
