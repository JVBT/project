Ford GT 2020

For the 2020 model year, the Ford GT received an update that introduced several mechanical upgrades and new special editions. The 3.5 L EcoBoost V6 is now rated at 660 hp (492 kW; 669 PS) due to gallery-cooled pistons and more powerful ignition coils.

Toyota F1

The Toyota FT-1 was a concept car that was unveiled at the 2014 North American International Auto Show. Its design was used as the inspiration for the production model of the GR Supra. Toyota's North American design studio, Calty, led the design process of this flashy sports car.
